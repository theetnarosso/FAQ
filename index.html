Reservations

Do you accept walk-ins?
Yes, we do accept walk-ins, but reservations are highly recommended to ensure availability.
How can I make a reservation?
You can make a reservation through OpenTable by clicking the “Reserve Now” button on this page.
What time does The Etna Rosso close?
Our kitchen closes at 9:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 PM on Fridays and Saturdays. The restaurant remains open as long as guests are seated and enjoying their evening.
What is your Late Arrival Policy?
You may lose your table after a 15 minute delay. Even if you inform us, we may not be able to seat your party. Each reservation has a time slot and we may not have a table later.
Best way to cancel a reservation
Cancel via the link in your confirmation email or text. If you don’t have access, text us at (954) 530-7910 with your name and reservation phone number.

Policies & Large Parties

Reservations & Dining Policy for Parties of 6 to 9 Guests
For parties of 6 to 9 guests, a minimum spend of $80 per person is required, including meal, tax, and gratuity. Cancellation must be at least 2 days in advance. Credit card holds of $35 per guest apply. Late cancellations or no-shows are subject to fees.
Can you seat more than six guests during peak hours?
During winter season, we can only accommodate groups larger than six before 5:45 PM or after 8:15 PM on weekends due to capacity and service flow.
Is there a dress code?
Our dress code is smart casual. We appreciate guests dressing appropriately.
Can I ensure that I am the only one who pays the check for my party?
Yes. Inform your server at the beginning of the meal and we can process the full payment discreetly with your card and preferred tip percentage.
Do you offer private dining or event reservations?
Yes, we offer private dining and event reservations. Visit the Private Dining page for more details.
Are children welcome?
We don't offer a kids’ menu, but we offer 30% off homemade pasta dishes for children 12 and under. Limited high chairs available.

Wine & Corkage

Do you offer alcoholic beverages?
Yes, we offer a wide variety of wines, including a red selection of special wines like Tignanello, Sassicaia, Solaia, Caymus, Far Niente and many more.
Do you serve cocktails?
No, we only serve wine and beer.
Can I bring a special bottle of wine?
We welcome outside bottles Tuesday through Thursday. Corkage Fee: $35 per 750ml, $46 per 1L. One bottle per reservation. No outside bottles on Fridays or Saturdays.

General Info

What are your opening hours?
Kitchen Hours:
• Tue–Thu: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM
• Fri & Sat: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
The restaurant remains open after the kitchen closes for seated guests.
How far in advance can I book a table?
Reservations can be made up to 21 days in advance. For further dates, bookings must be made in person.
What is your cancellation policy?
For 1–5 guests: cancel up to 24 hours before. For larger tables: non-refundable $35 per guest applies if canceled less than 5 days before.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes, please inform us when reserving and remind your waiter when ordering.
Is parking available?
Street parking is available. You may also park next door at Galt Realty after 5 PM. More free parking is available after 6 PM on Galt Ocean Dr in the back.
If the system shows “no availability”
If we receive cancellations or no-shows, our system may notify you. Text us at (954) 530-7910; we may be able to accommodate you.
