Reservations

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we do accept walk-ins, but reservations are highly recommended to ensure availability.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through OpenTable by clicking the “Reserve Now” button on this page.

What time does The Etna Rosso close?

While our kitchen closes at 9:00 PM on weekdays and 10:00 PM on Fridays and Saturdays, the restaurant remains open as long as guests are still seated and enjoying their evening.

After the kitchen closes, you’re still welcome to order desserts, coffee, wine, and other beverages.

What is your Late Arrival Policy?

You may lose your table after a 15-minute delay. Even if you let us know in advance that you are running late, we may not be able to seat your party. Every reservation has a time slot on the schedule and we may not have a table in a different time frame available.

Best way to cancel a reservation

Reservations can be canceled via the link in your confirmation email or text. If you don't have access to your confirmation, please text us at (954) 530-7910 with the name and phone number on the reservation.

Wine & Corkage

Do you offer alcoholic beverages?

Yes, we offer a wide variety of wines, including a red selection of special wines like Tignanello, Sassicaia, Solaia, Caymus, Far Niente and many more.

Do you serve cocktails?

No, we only serve wine and beer.

Can I bring a special bottle of wine?

We welcome outside bottles Tuesday through Thursday.

  • Corkage Fee: $35 per 750ml, $46 per 1L
  • Limit: One bottle per reservation (not per guest or per seating)
  • Important: No outside bottles on Fridays or Saturdays

Policies & Large Parties

Reservations & Dining Policy for Parties of 6 to 9 Guests

At The Etna Rosso, we’re honored to welcome your party. Given our intimate setting (about 30 indoor seats), please review the following:

  1. Minimum Spend: $80 per person (includes meal, 7% tax, 20% gratuity).
  2. Cancellation: At least 2 days in advance, by 5:30 PM before your reservation date.
  3. How to Cancel: Through OpenTable, via text (954) 530-7910, or in person (no voicemail/email cancellations).
  4. No-Show / Late Cancellation Fee: $35 per guest if not canceled per policy; charged 15 minutes after the reservation time or later that evening.
  5. Changes to Party Size: $35 charge per missing guest unless updated 48 hours in advance.
  6. Reservation Timing: Large groups available only before 5:45 PM or after 8:15 PM.
  7. Credit Card Hold: $35 hold per guest required to secure the reservation.
  8. Why These Policies: Our limited capacity requires careful planning to maintain service quality.
  9. Additional Note: No minimum spend for tables up to 5 guests.
Can you seat more than six guests during peak hours?

During the winter season, we can only accommodate reservations for groups larger than six before 5:45 PM or after 8:15 PM on weekends.

Is there a dress code?

Our dress code is smart casual. We appreciate our guests dressing appropriately.

Can I ensure that I am the only one who pays the check for my party?

Yes. Give us your card and preferred tip percentage at the beginning of your meal, and we’ll process payment discreetly at your signal.

Do you offer private dining or event reservations?

Yes. Please contact us in person or visit the private dining page for more details.

Are children welcome?

We don't offer a kids' menu, but we offer 30% off any homemade pasta dish for children 12 and under. Please note we have a limited number of high chairs.

General Info

What are your opening hours?

Kitchen Hours
• Tuesday to Thursday: 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM
• Friday & Saturday: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM

The restaurant remains open after the kitchen closes to accommodate guests who are already seated and enjoying their evening.

How far in advance can I book a table?

Reservations can be made up to 21 days in advance. To book further ahead, please visit us in person.

What is your cancellation policy?

1–5 guests: cancel up to 24 hours before the reservation time.

6+ guests: a non-refundable $35 per guest will apply if the reservation is not canceled at least 5 days before the reservation date.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we accommodate most dietary restrictions. Please inform us when making your reservation, and remind your waiter while placing the order.

Parking available?

Street parking is available. You can also park next door where Galt Realty allows our customers to park after 5:00 PM until 11:00 PM. More free parking is available after 6:00 PM on Galt Ocean Dr in the back.

If the system shows “no availability”

If we receive cancellations or there is a no-show, you can get notified directly by our reservation system. Don’t hesitate to text us at (954) 530-7910 as we may be able to accommodate you due to a cancellation or a glitch in the online system.
