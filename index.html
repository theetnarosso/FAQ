Reservations

Booking, walk-ins, late arrivals
Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we accept walk-ins, but reservations are highly recommended to ensure availability.

How can I make a reservation?

You can make a reservation through OpenTable by clicking the “Reserve Now” button on this page.

Policies & Large Parties

Groups, cancellation, minimums
Reservations & Dining Policy for Parties of 6 to 9 Guests

For parties of 6 to 9 guests, a minimum spend of $80 per person is required, including meal, tax, and gratuity. Cancellation must be at least 2 days in advance. Credit card holds of $35 per guest apply. Late cancellations or no-shows are subject to fees.

Wine & Corkage

Wine list, corkage fee, policy
Can I bring my own wine?

We welcome outside bottles Tuesday through Thursday. Corkage Fee: $35 per 750ml, $46 per 1L. No outside bottles on Fridays or Saturdays.

The restaurant remains open after the kitchen closes for seated guests.
